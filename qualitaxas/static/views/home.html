About Sandra Portales

Sandra Portales has been serving individuals and businesses offering accounting solutions for over 10 years.

She has a Bachelor’s degree in Accounting from Texas A & M International University and a Masters degree in Accounting from the University of Dallas. Additionally Sandra serves as a President on the Board of Directors of Desarollo Integral Latino Americano, an organization that assists with the job training and education for disadvantaged individuals.

Being raised in Mexico, she is fluent in Spanish.

Sandra greatly enjoys working with those who have businesses, helping them achieve their business goals. She is passionate about developing and creating unique ideas for individuals and businesses. Her specialties are bookkeeping and tax record solutions. She also assists business owners with organizational structuring and business networking referrals.

She loves what she does and enjoys working with every person she meets.

Sandra’s desire is to see your business and individual goals become a reality!
